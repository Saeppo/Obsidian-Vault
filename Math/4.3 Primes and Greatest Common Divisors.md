## Greatest Common Divisor 
![[Pasted image 20251107114041.png]]
Den største common divisor og Least Common Multiple skal bare være et heltal, og behøver ikke være et primtal

**Relatively Prime (to n):**  
A number a is relatively prime to n if **gcd(a, n) = 1**.  
They share **no common factors** other than 1.

- Example: Numbers relatively prime to 12 → 1, 5, 7, 11
    
- Quick check: Ignore numbers divisible by **any prime factor of n**.

**Pairwise Relatively Prime Rule:**  
A set of integers is **pairwise relatively prime** if **every pair of numbers has gcd = 1**.

**Important:**

- Do **not** confuse with mod values.
    
- Each pair is checked separately; there’s no single modulus that proves it.
    

**Example:** {21, 34, 55} → pairwise relatively prime  
**Counterexample:** {14, 17, 85} → not pairwise relatively prime (gcd(17,85) = 17)
